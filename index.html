Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about products, orders, and services.

Orders & Payments

How do I place an order? +
Browse products, add items to cart, and click "Proceed to Checkout". Follow steps to enter address and payment. You'll receive confirmation SMS after placing order.
What payment methods are accepted? +
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery for orders above ₹299.
Can I cancel my order? +
Yes! Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours. After that, contact support at hello@elitebeautyhub.in or call +91 63802 84958.

Products & Quality

Are your products 100% authentic? +
Absolutely! All products are genuine and sourced directly from authorized distributors. Strict quality check for every item.
Do you sell cruelty-free products? +
Many products are cruelty-free and vegan. Look for the 🌿 icon. We constantly expand our cruelty-free range.

Shipping Policy 🚚

Delivery timelines, charges, and tracking your order.

Delivery Timelines

🏙️ Metro Cities

Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai — 1–2 business days after dispatch.

🌆 Tier 2 & 3 Cities

2–4 business days for most cities across India.

🏔️ Remote Areas

4–7 business days for J&K, Northeast, Andaman, Lakshadweep.

Shipping Charges

🆓 FREE shipping on all orders above ₹999. Below ₹999, flat charge of ₹99. Express delivery ₹149 extra (metro only).

Order Tracking

Once shipped, receive SMS with tracking link. We partner with Delhivery, Blue Dart, and Ekart.

Returns & Refunds 🔄

Hassle-free 7-day return policy on most products.

Return Policy

Return most items within 7 days of delivery for full refund or exchange. Items must be unused, in original packaging.

✅ Eligible

  • Damaged or defective products
  • Wrong item delivered
  • Sealed unopened products

❌ Not Eligible

  • Opened/used cosmetics
  • Perfumes with broken seals
  • Clearance sale items

Refund Methods

Refunds credited to original payment method. UPI/card refunds in 3–5 business days. COD via bank transfer in 5–7 business days.
